About Us

Neethu's Spa Dubai

At Neethu’s Spa, we believe true beauty and wellness come from balance between body, mind, and spirit. Located in the heart of Dubai, our spa combines the timeless wisdom of Ayurveda with modern spa therapies to create a sanctuary of relaxation and healing.

Our mission is to provide every guest with a personalized wellness journey. From traditional Ayurvedic treatments like Abhyanga and Shirodhara to global therapies such as Swedish, Thai, Aroma, and Hot Stone massages, each service is designed to restore harmony, relieve stress, and rejuvenate your entire being.

What sets us apart is our commitment to natural healing. We use pure herbal oils, therapeutic techniques, and holistic practices to ensure that every treatment not only relaxes but also nurtures your long-term health and wellness.

With a team of skilled and experienced therapists, a tranquil environment, and treatments tailored to your unique needs, Neethu’s Spa is more than just a spa — it is a place to reconnect with yourself, release stress, and embrace a healthier lifestyle.

Step into our world of calm and let us guide you towards renewal, balance, and holistic well-being.
